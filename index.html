Data center electrical contractor. Record $5.5B annual revenue, $9.5B backlog. Also acquired Ansco/Overland wireless group ($150M). Expanding beyond telecom into AI/data center.

$13.8B trailing revenue, 38K employees. Continuing aggressive acquisition strategy across telecom and infrastructure.

Former large turnkey contractor, emerged from bankruptcy. MasTec and Dycom had also considered QualTek as target. Atlas positioning as major infra contractor.
Small contractor closures accelerating

CommScope: Sold OWN + DAS to Amphenol ($2.1B, 2025)
Outdoor Wireless Networks and Distributed Antenna Systems units divested. Equipment consolidation paralleling contractor consolidation.
⚠ Structural Forces Driving Consolidation

AT&T turf model elimination (June 2026) requires single-GC + one sub tier only. 50% NWSA certification mandates raise barriers. Net-90 payment terms squeeze working capital. Matrix pricing flat since ~2020. The combination favors larger, well-capitalized, certified firms and creates acquisition opportunities for companies with balance sheet strength.
